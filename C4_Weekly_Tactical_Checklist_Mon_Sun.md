# Appendix C4 — Weekly Tactical Checklist (Mon–Sun)

## Purpose
This checklist governs **weekly execution discipline** across marketing, prospecting, brokerage coordination, client service, and recovery. It ensures consistency, prevents silent backlog, and provides early detection of capacity strain.

This is a **governance checklist**, not a productivity wish list.

---

## MONDAY — Marketing & Infrastructure
- ☐ Record weekly videos / reels
- ☐ Write and finalize social posts
- ☐ Update landing pages and QR destinations
- ☐ Prepare pop-bys and print materials
- ☐ Design or advance quarterly mailers
- ☐ Schedule all weekly content
- ☐ Confirm Tuesday–Saturday prospecting plan
- ☐ 1-hour physical workout completed

---

## TUESDAY — Prospecting + Brokerage
- ☐ Morning outbound prospecting completed
- ☐ Street-level lead generation block completed
- ☐ Attend 1:1 meeting with Jarrett
- ☐ Log coaching takeaways
- ☐ Daily ops review completed
- ☐ 1-hour physical workout completed

---

## WEDNESDAY — Prospecting + Brokerage
- ☐ Morning outbound prospecting completed
- ☐ Street-level lead generation block completed
- ☐ Brokerage work block (10:00–12:00)
- ☐ Apply brokerage insights to pipeline
- ☐ Daily ops review completed
- ☐ 1-hour physical workout completed

---

## THURSDAY — Prospecting Depth
- ☐ Full outbound prospecting block completed
- ☐ Street-level farm engagement completed
- ☐ Business-owner relationship touchpoints made
- ☐ Appointment confirmations sent
- ☐ Daily ops review completed
- ☐ 1-hour physical workout completed

---

## FRIDAY — Prospecting + Pipeline
- ☐ Outbound prospecting completed
- ☐ Street-level presence maintained
- ☐ Pipeline status updated in CRM
- ☐ Follow-ups queued for next week
- ☐ Daily ops review completed
- ☐ 1-hour physical workout completed

---

## SATURDAY — Appointments & Light Prospecting
- ☐ Morning prospecting touchpoints completed
- ☐ Showings and appointments completed
- ☐ Client communications cleared
- ☐ File and CRM cleanup completed
- ☐ Daily ops review completed
- ☐ 1-hour physical workout completed

---

## SUNDAY — Weekly Reset (Personal Capacity)
- ☐ Weekly performance review completed
- ☐ Lead and pipeline review completed
- ☐ Marketing prep for upcoming week
- ☐ CRM cleanup and tagging
- ☐ Calendar planning completed
- ☐ Identify top 3 priorities for Monday
- ☐ Weekly athletic activity completed

---

## Weekly Completion Check

- ☐ No unresolved client issues carried forward
- ☐ No marketing or prospecting backlog
- ☐ Recovery and personal capacity protected
- ☐ Systems and files current

If any item above is unchecked for two consecutive weeks, trigger a **capacity review** (Appendix A5).

---

## Governance Notes
- This checklist is reviewed weekly, not sporadically.
- Missed items are signals, not failures.
- Chronic misses require scope or volume reduction.

---

## Executive Rule (Non-Negotiable)

If weekly execution consistency degrades,  
**growth pauses before standards slip**.
