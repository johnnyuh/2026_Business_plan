    # Appendix C6 — Weekly Review Template

## Purpose
This template governs the **weekly operational and capacity review**. It converts daily execution signals into structured insight, ensures early detection of drift, and informs growth and pause decisions.

This is a **governance review**, not a productivity recap.

---

## When This Review Occurs

- Conducted **once per week**
- Typically completed on **Sunday**
- Required even during light or disrupted weeks
- Takes approximately **20–30 minutes**
