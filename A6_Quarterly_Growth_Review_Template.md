# Appendix A6 — Quarterly Growth Review Template

## Purpose
This template governs the **formal quarterly evaluation of growth eligibility**. It ensures that growth decisions are deliberate, evidence-based, and aligned with the Growth & Scalability Framework.

This is a **decision document**, not a reflection exercise.

---

## When This Review Occurs

- Conducted **once per quarter**
- Scheduled in advance
- No growth actions are authorized outside this review
- Review must be completed in full, even if the decision is “no change”
