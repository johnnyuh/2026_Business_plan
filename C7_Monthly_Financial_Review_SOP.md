# Appendix C7 — Monthly Financial Review SOP

## Purpose
This SOP governs the **monthly financial review** to ensure visibility, discipline, and alignment between financial performance, funnel behavior, and capacity constraints. It is designed to support informed decisions without encouraging reactive cost-cutting or growth pressure.

This is a **governance review**, not an accounting exercise.

---

## When This Review Occurs

- Conducted **once per month**
- Scheduled on the calendar
- Completed even during low-activity months
- Duration: **30–45 minutes**

---

## Inputs Required

- Closed transactions for the month
- Pending and under-contract pipeline
- Consultation volume and quality signals
- Marketing execution summary (from B4/B5)
- Capacity signals (from C5/C6)

## Governance Notes

- Financial performance is interpreted **in context**, not in isolation
- Strong months do not automatically justify growth
- Weak months do not automatically justify contraction
- Decisions remain governed by Growth Gates (A3) and Pause Conditions (A4)

---

## Executive Rule (Non-Negotiable)

If financial pressure begins to distort **decision quality or advisory posture**,  
governance intervenes before tactics change.
