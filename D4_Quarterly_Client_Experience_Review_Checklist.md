# Appendix D4 — Quarterly Client Experience Review Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist governs the **formal quarterly review of client experience quality**. It ensures that experience integrity remains intact as volume, complexity, or market conditions change.

This is a **governance review**, not a satisfaction survey.

---

## When This Review Occurs

- Conducted **once per quarter**
- Scheduled independently of production or growth reviews
- Completed even if no issues are perceived
- Duration: **30–45 minutes**
