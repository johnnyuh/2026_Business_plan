## SYSTEM RULES
- One contact = one record
- Each contact must have a unique Contact ID
- Dates use ISO format (YYYY-MM-DD)
- Pipeline Stage values must match the definitions below
- AI must never duplicate records
- AI must preserve all existing records unless explicitly instructed to delete
- AI must rewrite the entire CONTACT RECORDS section after any update
- AI must not modify this SYSTEM RULES section unless explicitly instructed

## TAGGING CONVENTION
Tags are free-form, comma-separated, and used for querying.

Recommended categories:
- Timeline (e.g., 2025 Buyer, 2026 Seller)
- Geography (e.g., Solterra, Applewood, Broomfield)
- Motivation (e.g., Downsizing, Upsizing, Relocation, Investor)
- Strategy (e.g., Equity Play, Off-Market, Price Sensitive)
- Relationship (e.g., High Referral Potential, Influencer)
- Risk (e.g., Financing Unclear, Unmotivated)
