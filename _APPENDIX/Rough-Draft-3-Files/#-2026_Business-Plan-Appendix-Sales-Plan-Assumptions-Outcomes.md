# 2026 Sales Plan — Assumptions vs. Outcomes (Managing Broker Review)

## Purpose
This table outlines the core assumptions underlying the 2026 sales plan and the corresponding outcomes the business is designed to produce. It is intended to demonstrate capacity alignment, financial realism, and controlled execution within a solo-operator model.

---

## Production & Revenue

| Category | Assumptions | Planned Outcomes |
|-------|------------|------------------|
| Total Transactions | Solo-operator capacity capped intentionally | **12 closed sides** |
| Transaction Mix | Balanced exposure to buyers and sellers | **6 buyer / 6 seller transactions** |
| Average Sale Price | Conservative, market-realistic pricing | **$600,000 per transaction** |
| Total Sales Volume | Capacity-aligned volume target | **$7.2M total volume** |
| Buyer Commission Rate | Competitive, realistic buyer-side rate | **2.8%** |
| Seller Commission Rate | Full-service seller representation | **3.2%** |
| Buyer GCI | $3.6M × 2.8% | **$100,800** |
| Seller GCI | $3.6M × 3.2% | **$115,200** |
| **Total GCI** | Base-case revenue expectation | **$216,000** |

---

## Lead Generation & Conversion

| Category | Assumptions | Planned Outcomes |
|-------|------------|------------------|
| Lead Sources | Multiple sources, limited complexity | Small number of standardized funnels |
| Funnel Design | Simplicity over customization | Predictable conversion behavior |
| Lead Volume | Sufficient, not maximized | Supports 12-transaction base case |
| Conversion Focus | Qualification and follow-up discipline | Reduced fallout and execution risk |

## Client Experience

| Category | Assumptions | Planned Outcomes |
|-------|------------|------------------|
| Client Expectations | Demand for clarity and trust | Consistent, high-touch advisory experience |
| Experience Design | Managed system, not ad-hoc | Repeatable service delivery |
| Post-Close Strategy | Relationship preservation | Referral and repeat leverage |

## Capacity & Risk

| Category | Assumptions | Planned Outcomes |
|-------|------------|------------------|
| Operator Capacity | Single-agent execution | Controlled workload |
| Growth Approach | Conditional, not assumed | No silent overextension |
| Risk Awareness | Solo-operator dependency acknowledged | Reduced operational fragility |
| Review Cadence | Regular performance review | Early issue detection and correction |

## Summary Interpretation
- Assumptions are intentionally conservative and capacity-aligned  
- Outcomes are designed to be repeatable, defensible, and professionally sustainable  
- Growth beyond this plan would require explicit review and operational readiness confirmation
