# Tactical Daily Schedule (Weekday Operating Day)
**Applies:** Monday–Saturday (with day-specific overrides)

---

## Daily Anchors
- **5:00–6:00** Personal start (non-work)
- **6:00–12:00** Primary Work Sprint
- **5:00–7:00** Dinner / home block (protected)

---

## 5:00–6:00 — Personal Start (No Business)
- Wake, hygiene, coffee
- Light reset
- One-line “today’s win condition”

---

## 6:00–12:00 — Primary Work Sprint  
*(Structure varies by day)*

---

## MONDAY — Marketing & Content Production Day

### 6:00–6:15 — Launch Checklist
- Review weekly objectives
- Confirm content priorities and deadlines

### 6:15–8:15 — Marketing Production Block 1
- Record videos & reels
- Film market commentary
- Write social posts and short market reports

### 8:15–8:30 — Admin Micro-Reset
- Log content progress
- Queue edits or uploads

### 8:30–10:30 — Marketing Production Block 2
- Edit videos & reels
- Update landing pages
- Prepare pop-bys
- Design quarterly farm mailers

### 10:30–10:45 — Break + Light Inbox Triage
- Urgent client items only

### 10:45–12:00 — Marketing Production Block 3
- Finalize weekly content
- Schedule posts
- Prep QR materials and print assets

---

## TUESDAY–SATURDAY — Prospecting-Heavy Days

### 6:00–6:15 — Launch Checklist
- Review CRM hot list
- Define top 3 outreach outcomes

### 6:15–8:15 — Prospecting Block 1 (Outbound Focus)
- Calls, texts, DMs
- Sphere and nurture outreach
- Appointment setting

### 8:15–8:30 — Admin Micro-Reset
- Log touches
- Queue follow-ups

### 8:30–10:30 — Prospecting Block 2 (Hybrid: Outbound + Street-Level)
Includes:
- Walking farm neighborhoods to:
  - Door-knock
  - Hang fliers
  - Speak with homeowners encountered
- Sharing neighborhood market insights via QR-linked landing page
- Relationship-building with nearby business owners
- Working consistently from:
  - Farm-neighborhood coffee shop
  - Apartment complex work lounge
  - Neighborhood workspace

### 10:30–10:45 — Break + Inbox Triage
- Time-sensitive responses only

### 10:45–12:00 — Prospecting Block 3 (Relationship & Presence-Based)
Includes:
- Continued street-level lead generation
- In-person conversations and follow-ups
- Relationship deepening through consistent presence
- Appointment confirmations

---

## 12:00–5:00 — Afternoon Operating Blocks (All Days)

### 12:00–12:45 — Lunch + Recharge
- Protected break

### 12:45–2:15 — Transaction & Client Service Block
- Active deals
- Client updates
- Vendor coordination
- Resolve same-day blockers

### 2:15–3:30 — Appointments / Showings / Field Work
- Listing appointments
- Buyer consults
- Showings
- Overflow prospecting if no appointments

### 3:30–4:30 — Daily Ops Review + Note Dump (Required)
- Review performance and outcomes
- Identify missed opportunities
- Capture notes and ideas
- Return missed calls
- Process email
- File management and system review
- Preview next day’s priorities

### 4:30–5:00 — Buffer / Transition
- Wind-down
- Prep for dinner block

---

## 5:00–7:00 — Dinner / Home Block (Protected)

---

## 7:00–8:30 — Light Flex Work (Optional)
- Prep next-day prospect lists
- CRM cleanup
- Schedule follow-ups
- Light marketing tasks

### 8:30–9:00 — Shutdown Routine
- Write next day’s first outreach target
- Devices down

---

## Sunday (Personal Ops + Prep Day)
- Weekly review and scorecard
- Marketing creation and scheduling
- CRM cleanup and list-building
- Calendar planning and confirmations
