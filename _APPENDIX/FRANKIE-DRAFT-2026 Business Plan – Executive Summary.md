Tone: Professional, strategic, future-focused

## 1. 2026 Strategic Priorities
1. Establish dominant visibility and trust in West Arvada and Broomfield through consistent future-focused, design-forward marketing.  
2. Achieve **12 total transactions** (8 listings / 4 buyers; $257,600 GCI).  
3. Maintain **50–60% net profitability** on an expense budget of $20k–$25k.
