# Appendix A2 — Growth & Scalability Framework (2026–2028)

## Purpose
This framework governs **if, when, and how** growth occurs between 2026 and 2028. It is not an ambition statement. It is a **constraint-based governance model** designed to protect client experience, decision quality, and solo-operator sustainability.

Growth is permitted only when explicit conditions are met and is paused when defined signals appear.

---

## Growth Definition (Canonical)

Within this business, **growth is defined as**:
- Improved predictability of outcomes
- Higher quality of decisions and execution
- Greater resilience across market conditions

Growth is **not** defined as:
- Maximum transaction count
- Rapid expansion of scope
- Team size or management leverage

---

## Governing Principles

1. **Capacity Before Volume**  
   The business must demonstrate sustainable operating capacity *before* any increase in activity.

2. **Quality as a Gate, Not a KPI**  
   Client experience, advisory clarity, and execution discipline are prerequisites—not trailing metrics.

3. **Reversibility Over Momentum**  
   Any growth decision must be easily reversible without operational damage.

4. **Governance Over Optimism**  
   Growth decisions are made during scheduled reviews, never reactively.
